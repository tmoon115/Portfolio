Welcome to the UNG Computer Science Club!

About Us

The UNG Computer Science Club is a collection of individuals interested in computer sciences. Our main goal is to bring together UNG students to share perspectives, knowledge, and enthusiasm for computer science. The Computer Science club provides several educational, social, and networking activities each semester for students. Students can collaborate on projects, participate in workshops and seminars, and attend job fairs and networking events.

The club is open to all students, regardless of major, year, or experience level. We meet every Wednesday in Newton Oakes Center 243 from 5:30 – 7:30 pm for pizza and fun. Come join us!

Upcoming Activities

Technology Assistance

We hold monthly Technology Assistance Sessions at the Library Technology Center on Dahlonega campus on the last Monday of every month.
If you're struggling with a piece of software or technology or would like to help someone understand their technology, please come join us.

Project Workdays

Every second Wednesday of the month we hold a project workday. Students are encouraged to bring any personal projects they like and work on them with support from the group. These meetings are a good time for collaborating and helping each other with challenging subjects. Even if you've never touched computer science before, we can help you find a fun, accessible project that can get you started.

Movie Nights

Held about once every two months at UNGCS meetings, we take a break from the computer science and watch a fun movie together. Come join us for a fun group outing.
Our next movie night will be 10/31 for a fun Halloween night out!

Past Events

Presentation Days

On September 5th we held the second annual UNG CS Presentation Conference where students, professors, and guest speakers gathered to discuss topics in Computer Science. Students presented their research and learned about each other’s projects as well as had the opportunity to hear guest speakers and professors give talks on their concentration.

Portfolio Workshop

A semester tradition, the UNG CS Club holds a day long portfolio creating workshop. For a field that is constantly growing, there's a lot of competition out there. During our portfolio workshop students can come learn about platforms and tools for promoting their personal brand and creating a portfolio of work that they can show to future employers.

CS Club Cookout

Everyone needs to let of some steam. At the end of each spring semester we hold a cookout on the banks of Lake Lanier where we relax and have fun together as finals approach and the weather improves.

CS Career and Internship Fair

The Computer Science Career and Internship Fair welcomed many employers and students this year. Like any career fair, networking opportunities and potential employment offers abound. Join us again next year to meet representatives from some of the top tech companies around.
